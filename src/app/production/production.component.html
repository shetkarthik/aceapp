Constructing our uniforms.

This is where our uniforms start to take shape. Each garment is carefully sewn together using tried and tested techniques.

In contrary to most mass-produced factories where each part of the garment is assembled by a different person, our uniforms are constructed by the same person from start to finish.

Branding.

Your identity is integral.
We make sure that our garments have the customized logo and branding as desired by you. We use three methods to represent what you believe in:
- Embroidery
- Screen Print
- Sublimation Print

Finishing quality check.

The garment is given a final check to ensure that the measurements are as specified by the customer. Each uniform is also checked for construction errors or other irregularities.
The garment is given a quick ironing and packaged carefully before being shipped.

Shipped across the country.

Our uniforms are shipped nationwide using express shipping, and are delivered straight to the preferred address of the customer.
